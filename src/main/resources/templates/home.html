This tool is provided by the University of Hawai'i to allow a limited form of file sharing between UH faculty and staff. It also allows users affiliated with the University to share files with non-UH users.
There is a limit of MB on total upload size. You may use the service multiple times to upload a collection of files whose total size exceeds this amount.
